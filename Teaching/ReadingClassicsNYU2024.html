Arithmetic Geometry Seminar (Fall 2024)

Welcome to the NYU Arithmetic Geometry Seminar webpage! This semester we will be studying elliptic curves and modular forms.

Leaders: Jacob Monzel, Jake Zweifler, and Zeid Ghalyoun
Email: jcm9913@nyu.edu, jdz8883@nyu.edu, and zg2546@nyu.edu
Class: Wednesday 6:30-7:30pm (WWH 202)
References: Arithmetic of Elliptic Curves by Silverman (AECI), Introduction to Modular Forms by Diamond and Shurman (DS) (We will also use chapter 1 from Silverman II as a guide in the second half of the seminar)

Approximate Schedule

Week Topic Reading Remarks
1 Review of Varieties and Algebraic Curves Skim chapters 1 and 2 in AECI Summary of chapters 1 and 2 in AECI. The goal for next week will be to start reading about elliptic curves in chapter 3.
2 Weierstrass Equations, Group Law Sections 3.1-3.2 in AECI (start to look at 3.3) Discussion on chapter 3.
3 Elliptic curves, Isogenies Sections 3.3-3.5 in AECI Continued discussion on chapter 3.
4 Elliptic curves, Isogenies, Dual Isogeny Sections 3.3-3.6 in AECI Continued discussion on chapter 3.
5 Tate Modules Sections 3.7 in AECI Continued discussion on chapter 3.
6 Elliptic Curves over Finite Fields Chapter 5 in AECI Introduction to elliptic curves over finite fields.
7 Elliptic Curves over C Chapter 6 in AECI Analysis of elliptic curves over complex numbers.
8 Weak Mordell-Weil, Kummer Pairing, Descent, Mordell-Weil over Q Sections 8.1-8.4 in AECI Introduction to descent and Mordell-Weil theorem.
9 Heights, Torsion Points Sections 8.5-8.7 in AECI Discussion on heights and torsion points.
10 Minimal Discriminant, Canonical Height, Rank, ABC Conjecture Sections 8.8-8.11 in AECI Exploring the ABC conjecture and related topics.
11 Modular Forms and Modular Curves Chapter 1 in DS Introduction to modular forms.
12 Modular Curves as Riemann Surfaces Chapter 2 in DS Discussion on modular curves.
13 Dimension Formulas Chapter 3 in DS Introduction to dimension formulas.
14 Eisenstein Series Chapter 4 in DS Analysis of Eisenstein series.
15 Hecke Operators Chapter 5 in DS Discussion on Hecke operators.
