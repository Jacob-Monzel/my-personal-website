Jacob's Michelin Star List for Gluten-Free Food

Personal, extremely biased gluten-free ratings.
- ★★★ Life-changing. Travel-worthy.
- ★★ Worth a detour.
- ★ Very good if you’re nearby.
Jump to: ★★★ ★★ ★

★★★ – Life-Changing

United States

Maryland

City Restaurant What I Ate Notes
Baltimore Mission BBQ Brisket, fries, sausage, sauces Amazing brisket and fries. Amazing value as well. Easy 3★.

New Jersey

City Restaurant What I Ate Notes
Jersey City Buddy's JC Breakfast wrap, Fruity Pebbles pancake, sausage, fried eggs Stunning GF-friendly breakfast spot. Wrap + pancake combo is insane. Sausage and fried eggs elite. Absolutely 3★.
Jersey City Tino’s Artisan Pizza Gluten-free pizza Best gluten free pizza I’ve ever had. Zero stomach issues (very careful about cross contamination). Crazy amount of options.

New York

City Restaurant What I Ate Notes
New York City Risotteria Melotti Risotto & gluten-free cheeseburger Fully gluten-free kitchen. Elite risotto; surprisingly great cheeseburger.
New York City Brooklyn Bagel Sausage egg & cheese, turkey & cheese bagels Best gluten-free bagel in NYC. Perfect texture. Totally 3★.
New York City (Williamsburg) Mission Sandwich Social Various sandwiches My favorite sandwich shop of all time. Endless options, huge portions, pricey but worth it. My brother and dad loved it too. 3★.
