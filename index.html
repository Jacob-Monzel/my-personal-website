I am a mathematics PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania, interested in central simple algebras, Brauer groups, and period-index problems. I am especially drawn to their connections with algebraic and arithmetic geometry, and to applying computational methods in this area. I previously completed an MS at NYU under Fedor Bogomolov, studying Arakelov geometry.
